Monday, June 8   ☀️

7:00 PM · The PIT Mainstage (Flatiron)

VERN & Friends!

Veteran musical improv team Vern returns to The PIT to perform a fully improvised musical. Expect an evening of laughter, tears, and song, reminiscent of a Sondheim fever dream.

7:00 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

Just Laff Laff: ASL Improv Night

Join for a night of bold, playful, and wildly funny ASL improv. This show brings a unique and energetic performance to Caveat.

Tuesday, June 9   🎭

7:00 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

LiveWire Sketch Teams

Magnet's homegrown writers and performers bring brand-new sketch comedy every month. Catch fresh content from ensembles whose work has been featured at major sketch festivals.

7:00 PM · BCC Eris Mainstage (Bushwick)

Pop(ken) Culture 2 — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

After a successful first show, Philip presents a brand new hour of Pop(Ken) Culture. Expect fresh observations and comedy at the BCC.

7:00 PM · BCC Eris Deep Space (downstairs) (Bushwick)

Home Teams — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

This weekly house improv show features a rotating cast of primo indie teams. This week includes Pig Teams: Camelot, Bucket TV, and Improvised Columbo.

7:00 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

RISK! live show and story slam in New York, Theme: Discomfort Zone

The popular show where people tell true stories they never thought they’d dare to share in public. This month’s theme is 'Discomfort Zone'.

7:30 PM · The Rat (Downtown Brooklyn)

Up Against the Wall

A live show dedicated to hearing new works out loud. It's a chance to experience fresh material in development at The Rat.

8:30 PM · BCC T effhe BCC Pig Pen (Bushwick)

Honk Patrol Presents: Improv for the Revolution — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

Watch as four changemakers aim to elevate the improv form and inspire a revolution. This show promises a unique perspective on improv.

Wednesday, June 10   ✨

7:00 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Megawatt

Each week, Magnet's hand-picked ensembles perform high-powered long form improv. See a rotation of groups bringing unique styles to various improv forms.

7:00 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

Peer Revue

Experts give fun talks, then comedians improvise based on the talks, sight unseen. It's a unique blend of education and spontaneous comedy.

★ 8:30 PM · The PIT Mainstage (Flatiron)

Masc Mandate: Pride!

An improv show featuring butches, with all proceeds benefiting The Borderland Rainbow Center, an LGBTQ non-profit. The show promotes community care and mutual aid, featuring an opening set by Jo Sunday.

8:30 PM · BCC Eris Deep Space (downstairs) (Bushwick)

Chicken Big — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

As seen on 'America's Got Talent', Chicken Big performs weekly at the BCC. Expect a show from this recognized group.

9:30 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

Scattergood Presents Stressed for Success

Twelve Second City-trained performers present 90 minutes of brand-new sketches, live music, and guest performances. It's a substantial sketch show from a talented ensemble.

Thursday, June 11   🎶

6:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Musical Megawatt

A full night dedicated entirely to musical improv, showcasing everything you love about musical theater, all made up on the spot. Catch a rotation of new, never-before-seen musicals.

7:00 PM · BCC Eris Deep Space (downstairs) (Bushwick)

The Good Time — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

A fully improvised show based on one audience suggestion, featuring Chicago improv veterans who have performed at The Second City and iO Chicago. Openers include Rat Pageant, Christine and Andy.

★ 7:30 PM · The PIT LAB (Flatiron)

Bad Hamlet

This show guides you through the rarely performed 'Bad Quarto' of Hamlet, with side-by-side comparisons, powerpoints, and perhaps a song and dance. It's Shakespeare made by Bard-lovers for Bard-haters.

8:30 PM · BCC Eris Deep Space (downstairs) (Bushwick)

Demon Time — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

As featured in The New York Times, come join the freaks of the BCC for a night of mania. Expect all new wild-ass improvised sketches every week in this free show.

9:30 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

Sagematch

Four contenders present talks, and the audience decides the victor. It's a competitive and engaging format where you choose who prevails.

10:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Extra Credit

Graduates of the Magnet musical improv program, including current Musical Megawatt cast members, join forces for an impromptu, mash-up class show. Expect fresh musical improv talent.

Friday, June 12   🎉

7:00 PM · The PIT Mainstage (Flatiron)

Wigs!

A high-energy improv show where wigs drive the story, based on an audience-voted genre and suggested setting. Expect on-your-feet narrative storytelling from quick, brilliant actors.

7:00 PM · BCC T effhe BCC Pig Pen (Bushwick)

Weirdos: A Character Mic — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

2023 CBS showcase girlies Alise Morales and Jetta Juriansz invite you to present your weirdest three minutes of characters. It's a free open mic for truly unique acts, with a focus on 'the weirder the better'.

7:00 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

Vocabaret: A Tourney At Law

New York's best (and only) monthly variety show for language lovers. Expect a clever and entertaining evening focused on words and their use.

★ 7:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Premiere: The Improvised Musical

Featuring NYC's top comedy performers and instructors, this show turns an audience suggestion into a fully crafted musical on the spot. The cast boasts professional credits from Second City, Baby Wants Candy, HBO, and Comedy Central.

★ 9:30 PM · The PIT Fishbowl (Flatiron)

Silly Sh*t - Clown Edition

A one-hour clown chaos show for misfits, queer folks, and creative outsiders, featuring a lineup of clown, buffoonery, and character-based physical comedy. The cast includes Tallie Medel from Everything Everywhere All at Once.

★ 9:30 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

The Washington Roast

The viral hit comedy show where your favorite (and least favorite) politicians get scorched in savage, no-holds-barred roast battles. It's a topical and often cutting show.

10:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

CrimeProv: A Murder Mystery!

An improvised murder mystery show where the audience decides who was murdered and who the killer is. The actors then make it all make sense in this show described as 'Knives out, but so much dumber'.

Saturday, June 13   🌟

4:00 PM · Caveat (Lower East Side)

Modern Whitney

Comedians interpret art, then an expert reveals how close they got to the true meaning. This show blends art appreciation with comedic interpretation.

7:00 PM · BCC Eris Mainstage (Bushwick)

Deli Milk — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

DELI MILK is a brand new hour of sketch comedy. Catch it at the BCC before it 'ages poorly'.

7:15 PM · The PIT Mainstage (Flatiron)

Sotto Voce & Friends

An evening of story and song, featuring a fully improvised, sung-through musical with no spoken dialogue. The show partners with public school music programs, with student performances opening the night.

★ 7:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

The Armando Diaz Experience

The legendary, longest-running improv show ever, where a guest monologist shares true stories that improv all-stars bring to life. This show has been highlighted by the NYTimes for its daring shifts in perspective and playful creativity.

8:00 PM · The PIT LAB (Flatiron)

PIT presents: FRESH FACES

Showcases up-and-coming standup students presenting their 'tight 5' at The PIT. Features an opener 'What If' by Mary Carpenter.

★ 8:30 PM · BCC Eris - Mainstage (Bushwick)

Baby Wants Candy — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

One of the best known, longest running, and most celebrated improv shows in the world. They create a full improvised musical based on an audience suggestion.

9:00 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Meltdown: Ghost Girl & Tony Tony Tony

Two powerhouse sketch teams combine for an uproarious hour of sketch comedy. Expect high-energy performances from Ghost Girl and Tony Tony Tony.

9:30 PM · The PIT Fishbowl (Flatiron)

Zany Cats Comedy

Zany Cats Comedy is a stand-up comedy show featuring a lineup of funny comics from across New York City. A solid choice for stand-up fans.

10:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Something Strange: A Magic Cabaret Hour

A cabaret style variety show featuring mentalism and magic from Dan Titmuss and Nilson Burgos, with guest performers. Come to the Magnet to experience something truly strange.

Sunday, June 14   🌈

★ 7:00 PM · BCC Eris Mainstage (Bushwick)

Rainbow Riot — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

Rainbow Riot celebrates the joys of Blackness and Queerness in Comedy. Expect an evening of inclusive and vibrant performances.

★ 7:00 PM · The Second City New York Blackbox Theater (Williamsburg)

Compass Party: Second City’s Improv Ensemble Night

A night of improvisation and comedic acts put on by up-and-coming improvisers from The Second City Training Center. Ensembles present a mix of short form and long form improv.

7:30 PM · Magnet Theater (Chelsea)

Slow Play

Some of New York's best improvisers explore a singular world with earnest improvisation, using longer scenes to discover a dynamic reality. Expect patient and thoughtful improv.

7:30 PM · The Rat (Downtown Brooklyn)

Don't Tell

An adaptation of Edgar Allan Poe's 'The Telltale Heart', directed by Jennifer Restak and performed by Hana Roh and Jennifer Restak. This offers a dramatic and unique experience at The Rat.

8:00 PM · The PIT Fishbowl (Flatiron)

Larry McMurtry's Cowboy Cavalcade

A show by the PIT Fishbowl. Details are sparse but it offers a chance to experience a unique performance.

8:30 PM · BCC Eris Deep Space (downstairs) (Bushwick)

Faculty Lounge — Brooklyn Comedy Collective (BCC)

A brand new weekly stand-up show featuring beloved BCC stand-up faculty members. This week includes Zach Schiffman, Emily Walsh, Soph, and Saheim James-Patrick.
